## 🗣️ Talk Proposal Template

Submit under `proposals/talks/` using this format:

```markdown
# 📣 Talk Proposal: [Your Talk Title]

## 🧠 What topic(s) would you like to present?
(e.g., Responsible AI, Fine-tuning LLMs, Open Source Governance, Reproducibility in ML)

---

## 🕒 How long will you need?
(e.g., 15 min lightning talk, 30 min presentation, 45 min workshop, 60 min panel)

---

## 🌟 How will this topic support our values/mission?
Explain how your talk reflects our goals of shared learning, inclusion, ethical innovation, or community empowerment.

---

## 🌱 How will this topic improve the community?
What problem does it help solve or what gap does it fill for our members?

---

## 🎁 What will folks leave with?
(e.g., New skills, clearer understanding, resource toolkit, improved workflows)

---

## ☁️ Will you need cloud or other infrastructure support?
(e.g., GPU instance, shared JupyterHub, streaming support)

---

## 🧾 Will users need to create accounts or access any tools?
List any platforms, tools, or prerequisites attendees should know about.

---

## 💖 Are you interested in sponsoring this session?
(If you or your organization would like to support logistics, snacks, cloud credits, etc.)

---

## 📬 What's the best way to get in touch with you?
(Name, email, GitHub handle, or preferred contact method)
